Empowering Everyone in the Digital Age

Hi, I'm Aditya Tiwari – a student passionate about learning and teaching digital technology. My mission is to make technology accessible for everyone, so no one has to depend on others in this digital era.

About Me

I am Aditya Tiwari, a school student from Uttar Pradesh who believes that digital knowledge should be for everyone – whether fully sighted, partially sighted, or visually impaired. My goal is to create accessible, affordable, and inclusive learning opportunities so that every individual can walk together in the digital world with independence and confidence.

My Mission

  • To spread digital literacy across all communities.
  • To provide accessible content for visually impaired learners.
  • To offer free and affordable online courses.
  • To help people become independent in the digital era.
